About place

Tirupati is a sacred city in Andhra Pradesh, India, famous for its ancient temples and pilgrimage sites.
The most renowned attraction is the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, perched on a hilltop and dedicated to
the Hindu god Vishnu. Millions of devotees visit this temple every year, seeking blessings and offering their hair
as a sign of devotion.
Tirupati also offers other cultural and natural wonders, such as the Sri Padmavathi Ammavari Temple,
the Sri Govindarajaswamy Temple, the Kapila Theertham waterfall, and the Sri Venkateswara Zoological Park.
